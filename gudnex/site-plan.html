Home page

Who We Are

Gudnex Systems is a business located in Asesewa in the Upper Manya Krobo District in the Eastern Region of Ghana. For more than sixteen years of professional excellence and experience, we have devoted our time and expertise to building our brand and serving you by providing unparalleled secretarial and IT services that create lasting value for our clients.

We are committed to providing excellent professional services to our clients, as we continue to grow. We are delighted as a team as we work daily to bring the best solutions to our clients. We are even more thankful for the partnerships we have with our clients and the useful feedbacks we have received from you.

The team therefore appreciate your continuous trust in us as one of Ghana’s leading and trusted secretarial and IT businesses.

What We Do

Our secretarial service includes typing, photocopying, desktop publishing, graphic design and printing. We can carry out range of tasks, such as crafting résumés and typing individual letters. Additionally, we offer a convenient document scanning service which gives you digital copies of your documents.

Our IT services include web and software development and IT support services. We have expertise in developing professional-looking website and also developing useful software to serve the needs of our clients. Our IT support services give you solution for any unforeseen technical difficulties.

With excellence as our priority, we take pride in providing solutions to our clients with pleasant smiles. You can take a look at our detailed range of services on Our Services page.

Our Services Page

Professional Typing Services

Whether you need a single paragraph, a book, a brochure, a university thesis, or a program for your upcoming event, our friendly and highly skilled team is here to help you with all your typing needs. At Gudnex Systems, we deliver the best quality and yet affordable typing, design and printing services for a wide variety of documents, such as: cover letters, posters, project works, brochures, program leaflets, résumé/curriculum vitae, business letters, essays, school assignments, etc.

Most of our typing projects are delivered on the spot. In spite of this, the turnaround time may vary based on the length and complexity of the work. We can work from voice to handwritten notes and deliver the typed work in your preferred format and layout, such as the specific line spacing, font style, font size, PDF format, Docx format, and many more.

Photocopying

We can photocopy up to A3 size on 80gm paper. We can also enlarge and reduce the copies as preferred. Our copiers are duplex and hence can produce double-sided copies. Our digital photocopying service is available for a single or several copies in black and white, greyscale or full color.

Document Scanning

Our state-of-the-art equipment will help you transform your filing cabinets and paper files into stunning digital images or a text-searchable library of digital files. You will not only save money by converting your paper documents into digital formats, you also allow yourself or your employees with more time to focus on your core duties. Contact us now to receive a free quote.

Document Binding

Starting from a simple stapling to high quality comb binding, velo binding, ring binding or hardbacks, we got you covered. We can bind different types of documents, including books, reports, training manuals, brochures, proposals, notebooks, dissertations, project works & more.

Document Lamination

We laminate documents of different sizes, from letter-sized to A3 sizes, in matt or glossy. Improve the durability of your printed documents by laminating them. Laminate your documents with us today.

Photo Printing

We can print photos from your digital camera or smartphone. Have photos printed to high resolution to keep the memories you cherished and enjoyed.

IT Services Package

Our IT services include web and software development and IT support services. We can develop professional-looking website and software to serve the needs of your business. Our IT support services give you solution for any unanticipated technical difficulties.
